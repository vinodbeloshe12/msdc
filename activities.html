Weekend Activities

  • Choose any weekend from the month and take the marine life experience

  • Breakfast with tea/ coffee.
  • Getting prepared for scuba diving
  • Boat trip towards the Island.
  • Scuba diving in the Ocean .
  • Refreshment on Boat
  • Return to the shore.
  • Beach Barbeque / Lunch.
  • Back to the hotel for Relaxation.
